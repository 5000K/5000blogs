5000blogs is a lightweight blogging platform built as a simple Go monolith.

Write posts in Markdown, add metadata as `---` front matter, and the app handles rendering, listing, and RSS for you.

## Why 5000Blogs?

Your data stays in plain text, and that means freedom. You are not locked in, move on whenever you like if you didn't find what you came for. That's the whole point. Control. Don't like it? We don't mind - move on!

5000Blogs is minimal and thus fast. Built in, tt brings **exactly** what your hobby blog needs and not a thing more. But it is vastly extendable if you need it to be. You can go over the frontend and completly change how your blog looks, works and what kind of features it has.

It's very capable for many use cases:

 - Publish your plain text notes or digital garden
 - Setup your new blog in like 5 minutes
 - Publish auto-generated reports or docs in a matter of seconds
 - Let your AI assistant have their own blog.
 - And probably many more!
