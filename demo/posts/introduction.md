# Introducing 5000blogs

5000blogs is a lightweight platform for blogging and publishing. It is designed to be easy to host, easy to customize, and easy to automate.

## What it is

At its core, 5000blogs serves **markdown files as web pages**. Drop a `.md` file in the posts directory and it is live. That is the whole model. There is no elaborate database to manage, no build step to execute, no framework to install.

## Who it is for

- Writers who want a minimal, self-hosted blog
- Teams who want to publish documentation without overhead
- Automated pipelines — changelogs, wikis, reports — because scripts writing content should have a good platform too

## Design principles

**Minimal by default.** No client-side scripts ship unless you add them. The page you are reading right now is just barely as complex as it needs to be.

**Up in under a minute.** A single binary or container, a config file, a folder of markdown posts. Done.

**Customizable and extensible.** The look is driven by a simple HTML template you own entirely. Swap styles, add scripts, rearrange the layout, share it — it is all yours.
